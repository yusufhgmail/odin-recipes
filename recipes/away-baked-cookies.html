Away-Baked Cookies Recipe

Description

This recipe is awesome because it takes practically no work!

(Well... unless you count walking to the store and getting cookies as work..)

Ingredients

None! (Maybe a bit of cash, and shoes so you can walk to the store. But none of these are actually used for making the cookies.. unless you count "buying" as "making"..

Steps

  1. Walk to the store
  2. Get the cookies
  3. Walk home

Voila!

Now you have away-baked cookies!
